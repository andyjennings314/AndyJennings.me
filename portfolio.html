My Work

JATO Dynamics

I have worked with JATO on their flagship Carspecs platform since December 2013. For the most part, this involved creating, customising, and bugfixing web portals for various clients, with various combinations of CSS3, jQuery, and AngularJS. I also contributed application logic to the Development version of the site, and helped maintain legacy versions of Carspecs using c#.

Unfortunately, since Carspecs relies on proprietory code, I can't share it with you, so you'll have to enjoy the screenshots of my favourite customised portals.

The Entertainer

Between May 2012 and December 2013, I was the front end developer for The Entertainer's online team. This involved creating custom brand and category pages from Photoshop wireframes, and circumnavigating the limitations of the CMS with heavy jQuery use. Many of the pages I created have been since removed, but you can browse the following:

  • Action toys and figures
  • Careers
  • Scooter glossary

Andy And Tania

For my wedding, I built a simple Bootstrap-based site to act as a centralised point for information about the wedding. Afterwards, it was modified to act as a repository for photos, videos, and articles about the big day.

Sylvain And Saphia

As a wedding gift for friends getting married in June 2016, I offered to build a similar site to Andy And Tania. The couple required a dual-language site, and initially requested a password-protected site. I therefore implemented a simple AngularJS framework to handle content navigation and dual language support, as well as adding a new colour scheme to the existing Bootstrap framework.

CJ Modelling

My brother requested a simple site with a image portfolio and contact details for his modelling career. I took the opportunity to create a one-page full bleed site, with a simple responsive layout.
