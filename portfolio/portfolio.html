Where I've Been

JATO Dynamics

I have worked with JATO on their flagship Carspecs platform since December 2013. For the most part, this involved creating, customising, and bugfixing web portals for various clients, with various combinations of SASS-lead CSS3, AngularJS, and jQuery. I also contributed application logic to the Development version of the site, and helped maintain legacy versions of Carspecs using c#.

Unfortunately, since Carspecs relies on proprietory code, I can't share it with you, so you'll have to enjoy the screenshots of my favourite customised portals.

The Entertainer

Between May 2012 and December 2013, I was the in-house front end developer for The Entertainer's online team. This involved creating custom brand and category pages from Photoshop wireframes, and circumnavigating the limitations of the CMS with heavy jQuery use. Unfortunately, they have since replatformed their site, so many if not all of the pages I created have been since removed.
